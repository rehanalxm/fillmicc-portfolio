Who We Are

At Fillmicc, we specialize in professional photo & video shoots for brands, businesses, and creators. From capturing raw moments to delivering full-edited cinematic content — we bring your story to life, frame by frame.

Whether it’s a product shoot, a model portfolio, a brand launch video, a shop promotion, or a travel-based event — our team handles the entire journey: concept, shoot, and final video delivery ready for digital platforms or campaigns.
